What is Pentathon 3.0?
Pentathon 3.0 is a multi-event technical fest featuring Appathon, Datathon, Webathon, HackStreet, and Ideathon.
What is the team size?
Participants may compete individually or in teams of up to four members.
Who is organizing Pentathon 3.0?
Pentathon 3.0 is organized by Next Gen AI in association with SRM Institute of Science and Technology.
What is the registration fee?
₹349 per team for one event. ₹529 for two events under a limited-time offer.
Will OD and certificates be provided?
Yes. All participants will receive official certificates and On-Duty approval.
What is the last date of registration?
The tentative last date is 26th February.
What is the event timeline?
Pentathon 3.0 will be conducted from 18th March to 20th March.
What are the prizes?
₹10,000 (1st), ₹6,000 (2nd), ₹4,000 (3rd) plus internship and research opportunities.
